everything you need.
nothing you don't.

Built lean and focused. No bloat, no paywalls, no nonsense.

📋
list + kanban views
Switch between a clean list and a full kanban board. Drag to reorder. Your layout, your call.
🍅
pomodoro timer
Floating, draggable pomodoro widget. 25/5 cycles, session tracking, ring progress — built in.
📅
due dates
Custom date picker, overdue badges, smart filtering. Know exactly what needs attention today.
🔄
real-time sync
Firebase-powered. Your tasks sync instantly across every device — phone, tablet, desktop.
🔐
private accounts
Sign in with Google. Your tasks are yours only — nobody else can see or touch your data.
🌤
live weather
Nothing OS-inspired dot-matrix weather widget. Because context matters when planning your day.
